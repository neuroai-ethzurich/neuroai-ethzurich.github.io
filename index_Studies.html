Studies Advice: NeuroAI for ECTS

Here we post information about NeuroAI-related ECTS units at ETH Zurich. This ranges from lectures, lab courses to labs for BSc/MSc/semester thesis. For specific programs, we can additonally advise under which subject categories you can take these units to get ECTS as part of your degree program. We hope to expand our advice to further programs in the future.

Currently, we are able to offer advice for the following degree programs offered at ETH Zurich:

  • BSc in Electrical Engineering and Information Technology
  • BSc in Computer Science
  • BSc in Mathematics
  • BSc in Physics
  • BSc in Health Sciences and Technology
